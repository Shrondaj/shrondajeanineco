Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a session?

You can book a session through our contact page, by calling (602) 902-2677, or by emailing us directly. We'll respond within 24 hours to schedule your appointment.

Do you accept insurance?

We currently operate on a private pay model. However, we offer sliding scale options for crisis support and can provide documentation for insurance reimbursement where applicable.

Are sessions conducted in-person or virtual?

We offer both in-person sessions in Phoenix, AZ and virtual sessions via secure video conferencing to accommodate your needs and preferences.

What is your cancellation policy?

We request 24-hour notice for cancellations. Late cancellations or no-shows may be charged 50% of the session fee. We understand emergencies happen ‚Äì please communicate with us.

Is everything confidential?

Yes, we maintain strict confidentiality in all individual and group sessions, with exceptions only for situations involving harm to self or others as required by law.
