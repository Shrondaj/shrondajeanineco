Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I hear back?

We respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please call us directly at (602) 902-2677.

Do you offer virtual sessions?

Yes! We offer both in-person sessions in Phoenix and virtual sessions via secure video conferencing to accommodate your needs.

What if I need immediate help?

For crisis support, call our direct line at (602) 902-2677 or the National Crisis Line at 988. Both are available 24/7.

How do I become a member?

You can join by filling out the contact form above, calling us, or visiting our membership page for more details.
