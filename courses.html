Membership FAQ

Can I change my membership tier?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your membership at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

How do I cancel my membership?

You can cancel anytime by contacting us. There are no cancellation fees, and you'll retain access through the end of your current billing period.

Do unused coaching sessions roll over?

Unused sessions from Harmony and Majestic Life memberships can roll over for up to 3 months, giving you flexibility in your healing journey.

Is there a discount for annual payment?

Yes! Pay annually and receive 2 months free on any membership tier. Contact us to set up annual billing.
